Broccoli Stir Fry

Description

This is a simple stir fry recipe that can be made with chicken or other meat.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Chicken or other meat
  • Powdered or fresh ginger, grated
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil to fry with (vegetable, canola, or avocado oil)
  • Sesame oil
  • Sesame sees
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut meat into small pieces.
  2. Chop broccoli into medium to small sized pieces, to your liking.
  3. Place oil in wok and turn to high heat.
  4. Place sesame seeds into wok and stir fry them until golden-brown. Remove them from the oil and set aside for later
  5. Stir-fry the meat. Drain juices and add more oil as needed
  6. Add soy sauce, about a tablespoon.
  7. Add ths sesame seeds, sesame oil, and ginger.
  8. Add the broccoli. Cook the stalks a bit longer than the heads.
